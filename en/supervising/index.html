Supervising

Our supervising service oversees operations at the Customer’s site across all stages of drilling, cementing, and pumping, representing the Customer’s direct interests.

We ensure compliance with technologies and procedures, promptly adjust recipes and operating parameters, maintain shift and final reports, coordinate contractors, and enforce HSE requirements.

Why Choose Us?

  • Objective Assessment of Contractors

    You receive a full and unbiased evaluation of your contractors’ work quality.

  • Prompt Recommendations

    You always receive timely and effective recommendations to improve cementing technology.

  • Deadlines and Safety

    We reduce non-productive time and prevent accidents.

  • Well Integrity Improvement

    We enhance the quality and reliability of well cementing.

  • Cost Recovery

    You can recover costs associated with deviations from the work plan caused by poor cementing quality.

Services

  • On-site cementing audits performed according to the Customer’s technical assignment.

  • Monitoring of casing, liner, plug, and remedial cementing equipment during running operations.

  • Control of drilling fluid parameters and circulation cycles prior to cementing.

  • Monitoring of compliance with process regulations during cementing operations.

  • Sampling of water, dry cement blend, cement and drilling fluids, and spacer fluids.

  • Physical and chemical analysis of dry cement blends, cement and drilling fluids, and spacer samples.

  • Inspection of materials used for preparation of cement and drilling fluids, as well as spacer fluids.

  • Unscheduled inspection of service company facilities — warehouses, laboratories, and cement plants.

  • Provision of detailed reports, action plans, and recommendations to improve cementing quality.
