Calibration and Maintenance

Calibration and Maintenance of Laboratory Equipment

This is a crucial part of any testing laboratory’s life cycle. Each measuring instrument, testing, and auxiliary device requires timely maintenance and verification. Our specialists ensure your equipment remains compliant with industry requirements and standards.

  1. Upon calibration, a certificate is issued; after maintenance — a diagnostic and repair report.
  2. We perform regular maintenance and repair of equipment by OFITE, FANN, GRACE, CHANDLER, ATRISAU (GREATEST), and others.
  3. Our clients include Schlumberger, RN-GRP, Sildyukar Geological Exploration, Integra, and many other companies.
