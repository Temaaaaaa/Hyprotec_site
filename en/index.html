Our Services

About the Company

  • Innovation Center

    Provision of field services using an automated online drilling fluid monitoring system installed directly on the rig.

  • Scientific and Technological Center

    Independent laboratory testing of cement and drilling fluids as well as fracturing fluids (accredited by the Federal Accreditation Service — GOST ISO/IEC 17025-2019). Supervising and auditing of contractors, processes, and laboratories related to drilling fluids and well cementing. Expert evaluations and formulation design of technical fluids.

  • Professional Training Center

    Training courses and workshops on drilling and cementing fluids for industry professionals.

  • Trading House

    Supply of laboratory equipment with full warranty and post-warranty service across Russia and abroad.

  • Service Center

    Commissioning, repair, maintenance, and calibration of laboratory equipment.

  • STC Accreditation

    The laboratory in Novy Urengoy is accredited by the Federal Accreditation Service under GOST ISO/IEC 17025-2019 — ensuring accuracy and independence of all testing.

  • Corporate Culture

    We apply the best international and national engineering practices to deliver every project at the highest professional level.

Laboratory Equipment

HYPROTEC LLC regularly supplies American, Russian, and Chinese laboratory equipment and spare parts both for third-party companies and for its own operations. Our Scientific and Technological Center in Novy Urengoy hosts a large stationary testing laboratory, where—since 2020—daily testing of drilling, cementing, and hydraulic fracturing fluids has been performed.

As the company scaled its own Scientific and Technological Center, HYPROTEC faced the need to find reliable manufacturers of professional laboratory equipment. While fulfilling this task, we encountered several challenges, namely:

  • Changes in market conditions and the availability of Western equipment

  • Searching for solutions both in the domestic market and in partner countries

  • Assessing the reliability and accessibility of the supplied equipment
