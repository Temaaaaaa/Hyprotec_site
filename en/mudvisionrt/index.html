Technological Challenges

  • Limited Control Over Drilling Fluid Parameters

    Measurements are performed manually by drilling fluid engineers, which creates a conflict of interest.

    The density of data obtained from manual drilling fluid measurements does not meet the requirements for safe wellbore drilling as the rate of penetration increases.

System Capabilities Overview

Functional Description

MudVisionRT is a fully automated system that continuously monitors a wide range of parameters — from basic ones such as rheology, temperature, density, electrical stability, and pH, to advanced ones like filtration properties and retort analysis.

The system helps prevent wellbore instability, low differential pressure, cuttings accumulation, and other risks, reducing NPT. Its “set-and-forget” architecture eliminates conflicts of interest associated with manual measurements.

Automatic Analysis Every 10–18 Minutes

  • Temperature: 0–100 °C
  • Rheology: 600/300, 200/100, 6, 3 rpm
  • Gels: 10 s and 10 min
  • Models: n, K, PV, AV, YP, τy
  • Density: 0.9–2.8 g/cm³
  • pH: 1–14
  • Electrical stability up to 2047 V
  • Heating to the required temperature

All measurements are performed automatically

Features and Advantages of MudVisionRT®

  • Compatible with WBM, OBM, and SBM fluids
