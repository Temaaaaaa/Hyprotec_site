Equipment

The laboratory is equipped with more than 100 units of instruments from leading manufacturers such as Grace, Fann, Matest, Memmert, LOIP, Thermo Scientific, GreaTest, and OFITE.

Most of the equipment has no domestic analogues. The facilities enable up to 250 full sets of cement slurry tests and up to 650 sets of drilling fluid tests per quarter.

The laboratory operates in accordance with Russian and international standards: ISO 13500, ISO 10414:2008, GOST 33213-2014, RD 39-00147001-773-2004, ISO 10426, ISO 13503, ISO 10416, ISO 9001:2015.

Services

  • Development and support in implementing innovative technical and technological solutions to optimize and improve drilling technologies for mature fields as well as regions with complex geological and environmental conditions (UBD/MPD, HPHT/LPHT, lost-circulation control, SAGD, etc.).

  • Optimization of well construction fluids and approaches to prevent complications during drilling, completion, and wellbore cleanup/start-up.

  • Optimization and selection of chemical additives to improve the quality of cement slurries and drilling fluids.

  • Development of formulations for drilling fluids, cement slurries, and kill pills tailored to specific geological conditions.

  • Development and testing of spacer/buffer fluid formulations.

  • Incoming quality control of cement, cement slurries, drilling fluids, and spacer fluids.

  • Incoming inspection of materials for preparing cement and drilling fluids, including spacer fluids.

  • Advanced training for laboratory technicians and engineers in cement and drilling fluids.
